About

I'm Josh, a full stack developer with a strong passion and motivation to learn as much as I can and join the wonderful industry of web development, creating beautiful things and solving new and interesting problems along the way.

I've recently finished and intensive 12 week Web Development Immersive course at General Assembly in London. During the course I learned many exciting technologies such as React, NodeJS, Python, and much more. It was a very rewarding experience where I learned how to create and deploy full stack applications. I'm now in the search of new opportunities where I can keep that excitement going and continue to learn as well.
